Aryan Kaul is an MSCS student at Columbia University (May 2027) and a member
of Dr. Lydia Chilton’s Computational Design Lab, focused on agent-based systems
and human–AI trust. He works with Dr. Zhuo Zhang on VLM-based Android GUI
testing agents to uncover risky or malicious UI flows and is a Software
Engineer at Amazon on Kindle Mobile Platforms. His interests include human–AI
trust, accessibility, and Android UI systems.

**Focus / Interests**

- Agent-based systems and human–AI trust
- VLM-based Android GUI testing agents for risky or malicious UI flows
- Accessibility and design systems for mobile platforms
- Reliability and observability for Android/Fire tablet platforms (crashes, ANRs, SLIs, CloudWatch)
- Android UI engineering with MVVM, Kotlin, and Jetpack Compose

Open to research collaborations.
